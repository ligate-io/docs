One token, one economic surface. `$LGT` pays rollup gas, registration fees,
per-attestation fees, and routes value to both builders and the protocol
treasury.

<Warning>
  Draft outline. Numbers are governance-adjustable at launch. Final values land
  with mainnet.
</Warning>

## Supply

Fixed total supply of **1,000,000,000 $LGT**. No inflationary issuance
beyond the initial distribution schedule.

## Distribution

| Allocation           | Share | Vest                                                              |
| -------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Node operators       | 30%   | Emissions over 10 years                                           |
| Team                 | 20%   | 12-month cliff, 36-month linear vest from mainnet genesis         |
| Community + airdrops | 15%   | Event-based + emissions over the team / investor vest window      |
| Treasury (DAO)       | 15%   | Governed by holders                                               |
| Investors            | 10%   | 12-month cliff, 36-month linear vest from mainnet genesis         |
| Creator rewards      | 10%   | Streams over the team / investor vest window via emissions        |

## Protocol fees

Three classes of fee at the protocol level, all paid in `$LGT`.

### Registration

- `RegisterAttestorSet`, default 10 `$LGT`, 100% to treasury
- `RegisterSchema`, default 100 `$LGT`, 100% to treasury

### Per attestation

Default **0.001 `$LGT`** per `SubmitAttestation`. Split per schema: 0 to 50%
routes to the schema's builder address, the remainder goes to the treasury.
Schema owners pick the split at registration. Changing the split requires a
schema version bump.

## Value accrual

The protocol is permissionless. Any third-party schema pays the same fees
that Themisra, Mneme, and Iris pay. Every attestation in the ecosystem,
across every app, accrues fee revenue to `$LGT`. The token is not a claim
on a single product; it is the settlement asset for a category. If a single
flagship underperforms but other flagships or third-party schemas win,
`$LGT` value still accrues.

## Node and attestor rewards

Node operators share transaction fees and block rewards from the 300M Node
Rewards allocation, on a decreasing emission curve. Attestors stake `$LGT`
and earn per-attestation fees; equivocation is slashable (the `disputes`
module in v1 formalises slashing).
