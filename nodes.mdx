Attestor nodes (followers) sign Proof of Prompt receipts and post blobs
to Celestia. Running one on devnet earns testnet `$LGT` and positions
you as a day-one operator when mainnet opens.

Reference shape from the public devnet deploy runbook
([`public-devnet-deploy.md`](https://github.com/ligate-io/ligate-chain/blob/main/docs/development/public-devnet-deploy.md)):

| Role      | GCP shape       | vCPU | RAM   | Boot disk | Data disk |
| --------- | --------------- | ---- | ----- | --------- | --------- |
| Sequencer | `e2-standard-4` | 4    | 16 GB | 20 GB SSD | 150 GB SSD |
| Follower  | `e2-medium`     | 2    | 4 GB  | 20 GB SSD | 100 GB SSD |

OS: **Ubuntu 24.04 LTS** (the released `ligate-node` binary links against
GLIBC 2.39; Debian 12 ships GLIBC 2.36 and won't load it without
rebuilding from source).

Full operator guide (Docker, Grafana, stake + slashing, runbook) lands
in the next migration pass.
