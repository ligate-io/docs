Attestor nodes sign Proof of Prompt receipts and post blobs to Celestia.
Running one on devnet earns testnet `$LGT` and positions you as a
day-one operator when mainnet opens. Hardware bar is low (2 vCPU,
4 GB RAM, 40 GB SSD).

Full operator guide (Docker, Grafana, stake + slashing, runbook) lands
in the next migration pass.
